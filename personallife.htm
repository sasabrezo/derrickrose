Personal life

His agent is former Bulls guard B. J. Armstrong.

Rose is a Christian. Rose has spoken about his faith saying, "... God does everything for a reason." Rose wears a wristband that says "In Jesus Name I Play" and has several tattoos about his faith.

On October 9, 2012, Rose's girlfriend, Mieka Reese, gave birth to their son, Derrick Jr.

In 2016, Rose was involved in a federal civil lawsuit to "assess whether he and two friends raped his former girlfriend." On October 19 of that year, he was cleared by an eight-member jury and absolved of wrongdoing.
